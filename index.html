FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything you need to know before you buy.

Will it fall off my window?

No. The robot uses 2800Pa industrial-grade vacuum suction that maintains contact even on slightly inclined surfaces. Additionally, it comes with a safety tether wire as a backup — so it's double-protected against falls.

What surfaces can it clean?

The robot works on any smooth, flat glass surface — home windows, office glass, mirrors, glass shower doors, glass table tops, and smooth ceramic tiles. It is not suitable for textured, frosted, or curved glass.

How long does the battery last?

The robot runs for up to 60 minutes per charge, which is enough to clean 8–12 standard windows depending on size. It charges fully in about 3 hours via the included USB-C charging cable.

Do I need to add my own cleaning solution?

Yes — you add your preferred cleaning solution (or just water) to the 100ml built-in reservoir. We recommend a diluted window cleaning solution for the best streak-free results. The robot distributes it evenly as it moves.

How loud is it?

The robot operates at under 55 decibels — quieter than a normal conversation. You can easily watch TV or work in the same room without any distraction while it cleans.

What's in the box?

In the box: 1× Robot Window Cleaner, 2× Microfiber cleaning pads, 1× USB-C charging cable, 1× Safety tether wire, 1× Remote control, and 1× User manual. Everything you need to get started immediately.
